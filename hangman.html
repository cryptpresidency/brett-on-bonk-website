How to Play Hangman
  • Guess the word by clicking the on-screen letters.
  • Each correct letter gives you +1 point.
  • You have 10 wrong guesses per word. If you run out, a new word appears.
  • Guess the full word to earn +30 seconds (max 5 minutes).
  • The game ends when the timer runs out.
  • Try to get the highest score before time is up!
